# Welcome to VeriWorkly

**VeriWorkly** is a free and open-source resume builder designed to simplify the process of creating, updating, and exporting your professional resume without compromising your privacy.

![VeriWorkly Banner](/og/landing-page-og.png)

---

## What is VeriWorkly?

VeriWorkly is built around a simple idea: career tools should not force people to trade privacy for utility. Most resume builders gatekeep your data, force you into subscriptions, or silently mine your information. VeriWorkly operates **100% local-first by default**, requiring no accounts, no mandatory cloud storage, and zero friction.

## Key Features

<Accordions>
  <Accordion id="free-open-source" title="Completely Free & Open Source">
    VeriWorkly is licensed under MIT. You can use it for free, modify it, and even host your own instance. The entire codebase is available on [GitHub](https://github.com/Gautam25Raj/veriworkly-resume).
  </Accordion>

<Accordion id="no-account" title="No Account Required">
  Jump straight into the editor and start building. Accounts are completely optional and only needed
  if you want to use cloud sync or managed sharing features.
</Accordion>

<Accordion id="rich-customization" title="Rich Customization">
  Adjust colors, fonts, spacing, section order, and content visibility. Tailor your master profile
  for specific roles in seconds.
</Accordion>

  <Accordion id="self-hostable" title="Self-Hosting Ready">
    Deploy your own instance of VeriWorkly using Docker. Keep complete control over your data and services.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
