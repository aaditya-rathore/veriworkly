# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Professionals, designers, engineers, and independent builders who need to turn their work into a credible public portfolio and keep it current without managing a custom website.

## Product Purpose

VeriWorkly Portfolio helps users shape, preview, publish, and understand a professional portfolio from one focused workspace. Success means a user can move from an incomplete draft to a clear, shareable body of work without fighting the editor.

## Brand Personality

Precise, confident, and creatively opinionated. Public pages should make portfolio building feel aspirational. Product surfaces should feel calm, fast, and trustworthy.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS landing pages, decorative glass dashboards, card-heavy admin templates, and portfolio builders that hide editing behind ambiguous controls. Do not make the workspace look like a second Studio documents app.

## Design Principles

- Put the work first: previews and portfolio proof should dominate.
- Make progress legible: users should always understand readiness and the next useful action.
- Keep editing direct: structure, content, and live output stay visibly connected.
- Separate expression from operation: public pages can be bold; workspace controls stay restrained.
- Preserve VeriWorkly trust: familiar navigation, clear language, and dependable states.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA contrast and keyboard behavior. Respect reduced motion, keep focus states visible, avoid color-only status communication, and maintain usable layouts from mobile through large desktop screens.
